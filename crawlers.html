Our Care

What We Offer

Designed to support your baby's growing curiosity, mobility, and social awareness.

Safe Exploration Space

Our classroom is specially designed with soft, padded areas that allow babies to safely explore their environment as they develop their crawling abilities.

Motor Skill Development

We provide activities specifically designed to strengthen gross and fine motor skills as babies learn to navigate their world through crawling, pulling up, and early standing.

Sensory Experiences

Age-appropriate sensory activities engage babies' natural curiosity and help them develop an understanding of different textures, sounds, and visual experiences.

Early Communication

We encourage early language development through songs, stories, and interactive play, helping babies begin to understand and respond to verbal cues.

Cognitive Growth

Activities like simple puzzles, object permanence games, and cause-and-effect toys help develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Social Interaction

While maintaining individual care, we begin to introduce opportunities for parallel play and social awareness as babies become more aware of their peers.

A Typical Day

Daily Schedule

Each Crawler has their own individual schedule. As every child develops at their own pace, we tailor each crawler's daily routine to match their unique needs, energy levels, and developmental stage.
