Walkers

Walking Toddlers

Our walkers classroom is designed for active toddlers who are mastering walking and developing language skills. We introduce more structured activities while still allowing for plenty of free play and exploration to nurture their growing independence. Our program focuses on developing communication, social skills, and early cognitive concepts through engaging, play-based experiences.

Our Care

What We Offer

A vibrant program that nurtures independence, creativity, and early learning through play.

Active Learning Environment

Our classroom is specially designed for active toddlers, with plenty of space to practice walking, running, climbing, and other gross motor skills in a safe environment.

Language Development

We emphasize language development through conversations, storytelling, songs, and interactive activities that encourage verbal expression and comprehension.

Social Skills

Toddlers begin to develop important social skills like sharing, taking turns, and cooperative play through guided interactions with peers and nurturing staff.

Creative Expression

Art, music, and imaginative play activities allow toddlers to express themselves and develop creativity in a supportive and encouraging atmosphere.

Independence & Self-Help

We foster growing independence through age-appropriate activities like self-feeding, simple dressing skills, and other self-help routines that build confidence.

Pre-Academic Foundations

Through play-based activities, we introduce early concepts in literacy, numeracy, and science that will form the foundation for future academic learning.
