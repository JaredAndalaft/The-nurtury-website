About Us

About The Nurtury

We are a small independent English-speaking ECD facility offering a friendly, clean and comfortable environment for babies and toddlers to learn and grow.

Open-Door Policy

Parents may come and view the baby centre at any time.

Our Philosophy: At The Nurtury we respect and nurture the culture, spirit, dignity and individuality of each individual child. The Nurtury is divided into three sections according to the developmental milestones reached: Infants, Crawlers and Walkers.

We cater specifically for infants and toddlers. Our age range is 4 weeks to 3 years. Please note that this means once your child turns 3, they will need to move over to a 'big boy/girl' school.

Our Programs

Our Classrooms

Five dedicated classrooms tailored to each stage of your child's developmental journey.

Age: 4 Weeks +

Infants Classroom

"We believe in nurturing each child's individual development journey at their own pace."

Our infant classroom provides a nurturing and stimulating environment for babies from 4 weeks old. Each baby receives individualized care with a focus on comfort, security, and gentle stimulation.

  • 1:4 staff ratio (better than legal 1:6)
  • Individual cots with changing stations
  • Weekly baby massage
  • Age-appropriate stimulation
  • Secure camera cot interaction
  • No crying policy

Crawlers Classroom

"Safe exploration and discovery for your growing little one."

As babies begin to crawl and explore their surroundings, our crawlers classroom offers a safe space with appropriate challenges to encourage mobility, coordination, and curiosity.

  • Safe, padded exploration areas

Walkers Classroom

"Building independence and confidence through play-based learning."

Our walkers classroom is designed for active toddlers who are mastering walking and developing language skills. We introduce more structured activities while still allowing for plenty of free play and exploration.

  • Active learning environment
  • Language development focus
  • Social skills building
  • Creative expression activities
  • Independence & self-help skills
  • Pre-academic foundations

Runners Classroom

"Channelling boundless energy into growth, learning, and fun."

Our runners classroom is designed for energetic toddlers who are confident on their feet and ready for more active, structured play. We provide a stimulating environment that channels their energy into developmental activities and creative exploration.

Jumpers Classroom
