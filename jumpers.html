Jumpers

Adventurous Toddlers

Our jumpers classroom is for the most adventurous and confident toddlers who are ready for advanced physical challenges, problem-solving activities, and pre-school preparation. We focus on building resilience, independence, and a love of learning that will carry them forward.

Our Care

What We Offer

An advanced program for confident toddlers ready to leap into the next stage of their development.

Advanced Physical Skills

Jumping, climbing, balancing, and obstacle courses that challenge coordination and build physical confidence in a safe environment.

Confidence Building

Activities that encourage toddlers to try new things, take safe risks, and develop the self-confidence they need for the transition to big school.

Pre-School Readiness

Structured learning activities that introduce early literacy, numeracy, and problem-solving concepts to prepare children for their next educational step.

Leadership & Teamwork

Group projects and collaborative activities that develop leadership skills, empathy, conflict resolution, and the ability to work together.

Creative Expression

Advanced art, music, dance, and dramatic play that encourage individuality, self-expression, and creative problem-solving.

Discovery & Science

Hands-on experiments, nature exploration, and discovery activities that spark curiosity and develop an understanding of the world around them.
